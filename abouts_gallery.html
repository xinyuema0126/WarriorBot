What can Warrior Bot help me with?

Co-op Statistics

Want to learn about the numbers through it all? Whether it be the number of applications you’re allowed to send, or when the certain deadlines are, ask Warrior Bot about any stats you may want to know about co-op.

Application Process

Confused about how to apply? Warrior Bot can guide you all throughout. From the start where you create your applications, to the middle where you try and finalize a position, as well as the steps that come after accepting an offer.

WaterlooWorks

With the vast majority of functions and services provided on WaterlooWorks, it is very easy to get confused and have a bunch of questions. Warrior Bot can help answer your questions about navigating it, its features, or even just general questions.

Working Internationally

With the ongoing pandemic, many Waterloo students are stuck all over the world. With co-op terms coming and going, it is only natural that many students have questions about working internationally and the legality of certain situations. Warrior Bot is there to answer all questions pertaining to this topic as well.
